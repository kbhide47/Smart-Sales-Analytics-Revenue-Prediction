BUSINESS GLOSSARY

Sales

Revenue earned from selling products.

Profit

Revenue after deducting costs.

Revenue

Income generated from business operations.

Discount

Reduction applied to product price.

Category

Main classification of products.

Sub Category

Detailed classification within a category.

Region

Geographical business area.

Segment

Customer classification.

Quantity

Number of units purchased.

Delivery Days

Number of days required to deliver an order.

Machine Learning

Algorithms that learn from historical data.

Prediction

Estimated future sales.

Dashboard

Visual interface displaying business metrics.

Business Intelligence

Using data to improve decision making.

KPI

Key Performance Indicator.

Forecasting

Predicting future business performance.

Inventory

Stock available for sale.
