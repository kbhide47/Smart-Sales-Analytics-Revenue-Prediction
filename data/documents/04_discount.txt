DISCOUNT ANALYSIS

Discount is the reduction applied to the selling price.

Small discounts can increase sales.

Very high discounts can reduce profit.

Business should avoid unnecessary discounts.

Discount should be used during:

Seasonal sales

Festival offers

Inventory clearance

Customer loyalty campaigns

Large discounts may increase revenue but decrease overall profitability.

Business managers should compare sales growth with profit before increasing discounts.

High discounts should only be offered for products with sufficient profit margins.

Discount strategy should balance customer attraction and business profitability.

Machine Learning considers discount as one of the important prediction features.

Increasing discount generally lowers expected profit.

Businesses should regularly monitor products with high discounts and low profits.
