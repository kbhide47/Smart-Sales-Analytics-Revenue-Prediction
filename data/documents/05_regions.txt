REGIONAL BUSINESS ANALYSIS

The dashboard divides business into four regions.

West

Generally generates the highest profit.

Often performs well due to strong customer demand.

East

Usually generates high sales.

Profit remains moderate.

Central

Some products generate losses.

Requires careful inventory planning.

South

Produces stable sales.

Generally maintains consistent profitability.

Regional comparison helps management allocate marketing budgets.

Managers should invest more in profitable regions while improving weaker regions.

Regional analysis supports expansion decisions.

Business should compare sales and profit together instead of only sales.
