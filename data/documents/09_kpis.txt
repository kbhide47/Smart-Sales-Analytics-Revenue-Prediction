KEY PERFORMANCE INDICATORS

Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) measure business performance.

Total Sales

Represents total revenue generated from all customer orders.

Higher sales indicate better revenue generation.

Total Profit

Represents the net earnings after deducting costs.

Profit is more important than sales because it reflects business sustainability.

Total Orders

Represents the number of completed customer orders.

A high order count generally indicates strong customer engagement.

Sales by Category

Shows which product categories generate the highest revenue.

Profit by Category

Shows which categories contribute the most profit.

Region-wise Sales

Compares revenue generated across different regions.

Monthly Sales Trend

Shows how sales change over time.

KPIs help management make informed business decisions.

Business managers should monitor KPIs regularly.

KPIs help identify opportunities and business risks.

Executive dashboards should always display KPIs clearly.
