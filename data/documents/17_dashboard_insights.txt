DASHBOARD INSIGHTS

Business dashboards provide real-time visibility into company performance.

Important dashboard insights include:

Highest Sales Category

Highest Profit Category

Most Profitable Region

Lowest Profit Region

Top Selling Products

Top Loss Making Products

Monthly Sales Growth

Monthly Profit Growth

Business managers should compare trends rather than individual values.

Sales growth with falling profit indicates excessive discounts or high operational costs.

High profit with stable sales indicates good pricing strategy.

Managers should investigate sudden changes in regional performance.

Dashboard insights support executive decision making.

Interactive dashboards improve business intelligence.

Businesses should monitor dashboard metrics daily.
