DISCOUNT STRATEGY

Discounts attract customers but reduce profit margins.

Large discounts should only be applied when:

Inventory is high.

Seasonal clearance is required.

New customer acquisition is targeted.

Small discounts often improve conversion rates without significantly reducing profitability.

Businesses should monitor:

Average Discount

Average Profit

Sales Growth

Customer Retention

A profitable business balances sales growth with healthy profit margins.

Discount optimization is a key business strategy.
