SMART SALES ANALYTICS DASHBOARD

The Smart Sales Analytics Dashboard helps businesses monitor sales, profit and customer trends.

The dashboard includes:

Total Sales

Total Profit

Total Orders

Sales by Category

Profit by Category

Region Analysis

Monthly Sales Trend

Top Products

Top Loss Products

SQL Analytics

Machine Learning Prediction

AI Business Assistant

The dashboard is built using Python, Streamlit, SQL, Machine Learning, LangChain, FAISS and OpenRouter.

The dashboard is intended for business managers, analysts and executives.
